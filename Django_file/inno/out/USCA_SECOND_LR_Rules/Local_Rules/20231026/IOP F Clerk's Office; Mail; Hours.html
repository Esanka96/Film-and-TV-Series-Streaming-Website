IOP F. Clerk's Office; Mail; Hours

The clerk's office is located at 40 Foley Square, New York, New York 10007, and the mailing address is the Thurgood Marshall United States Courthouse, 40 Foley Square, New York, New York 10007. The office is open from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily, except Saturdays, Sundays, legal holidays, and any other days the chief judge may designate.
