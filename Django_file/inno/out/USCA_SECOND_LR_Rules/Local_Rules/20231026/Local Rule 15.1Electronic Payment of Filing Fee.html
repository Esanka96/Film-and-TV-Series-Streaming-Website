Local Rule 15.1. Electronic Payment Of Filing Fee

If the petitioner is represented by counsel, the petitioner's attorney may remit the filing fee to the circuit clerk electronically in accordance with the instructions posted on the court's website. The attorney must (1) register as a Filing User under LR 25.1, (2) file the petition for review electronically with the fee, and (3) if not already admitted, seek admission to the court under LR 46.1 immediately upon filing the petition for review.
