Local Rule 4.2. Duties Regarding Trial Court Motions

A party who has filed a notice of appeal must promptly notify this Court when a motion referenced in FRAP 4(a)(4), 4(b)(3), or 6(b)(2) is filed, and must notify this court within 14 days after entry of the order disposing of the last such remaining motion.
